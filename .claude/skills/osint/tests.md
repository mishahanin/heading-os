# Test Prompts: osint

## Should Trigger (positive)
Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "dig into the legacy incumbent for me, I want everything -- financials, leadership, recent deals, where they're weak"
- "who is this guy Omar Khan from ExampleTelco? background check before Thursday's call"
- "what do we know about a competing vendor Networks? they keep coming up in RFPs"
- "I need a full dossier on ExampleTelco's DPI stack and who runs their security division"
- "due diligence on this company a competing vendor -- they might be acquiring someone in our space"
- "deep dive on the UAE cybersecurity market, who are the real players"
- "find out everything about a competitor, especially their telco contracts in [region]"
- "intel on the CTO of Nimbus Telecom, meeting him next week"
- "recon on this startup called Palo Alto spinoff doing network analytics -- forgot their name but they raised series B last year"
- "profile the decision makers at Meridian Mobile for network intelligence procurement"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "send an email to Omar about the proposal" (email task, not intelligence)
- "add the legacy incumbent to the CRM as a competitor" (CRM operation)
- "write a LinkedIn post about sovereign DPI" (content creation)
- "google their phone number real quick" (simple lookup, not deep investigation)
- "what's the weather today" (general question)
