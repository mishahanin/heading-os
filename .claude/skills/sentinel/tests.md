Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "start sentinel"
- "is sentinel running?"
- "sentinel status"
- "stop the monitor"
- "check sentinel logs -- any alerts today?"
- "restart sentinel, I think it crashed"
- "what has sentinel flagged in the last 24 hours"

- "configure sentinel to check every 10 minutes instead of 15"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "check my email for anything urgent" (direct email check, not sentinel management)
- "read my telegram messages" (telegram skill, not sentinel)
- "set up a monitoring dashboard" (dashboard or general task)
- "what's happening -- any news?" (ceo-intel, not sentinel)
- "alert me when the ExampleTelco email comes in" (not how sentinel works)
