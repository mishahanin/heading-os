# 31C Terminology & Operational Vocabulary

Last Verified: 2026-05-15

## Required Terms

- **Tribe / 31C Tribe** -- The company's people. NEVER use "team," "family," or "crew."
- **ODUN.ONE** -- The sovereign deep packet intelligence platform. Tagline: "From Deep Packet Inspection to Deep Packet Intelligence."
- **DPI+** -- Next-generation Deep Packet Intelligence (beyond traditional DPI).
- **The Navigation Principle** -- 31C's core operating philosophy. Frame priorities as operational states, not rigid targets. Use maritime vocabulary where natural.

## Operational Vocabulary

Use correctly in all outputs:

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Operational State** | A sustained condition the Tribe maintains -- not a target to hit |
| **Heading** | Direction of movement -- adjustable, unlike a fixed destination |
| **Sea State** | External conditions affecting operations |
| **Course Correction** | Adjustment to behavior/rhythm that restores an operational state |
| **Drift** | Unconscious movement out of an operational state |
| **State Check** | Diagnostic assessment replacing traditional reviews (15 min max) |
| **Crunch Mode** | Heightened operational state -- compressed decisions, tightened communication, zero drift tolerance |

## Five Core Principles

Foundational operational states, not rules:

1. Proof of Value over PoC
2. Partnership for Life
3. Operate with Integrity
4. Deliver Under Pressure
5. Data Sovereignty Always
