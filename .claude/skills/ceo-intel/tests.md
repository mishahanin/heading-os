# Test Prompts: ceo-intel

## Should Trigger (positive)
Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "what's happening in the world today"
- "morning intel"
- "give me the world brief"
- "ceo intel -- what should I know before my day starts"
- "anything critical going on globally that affects us?"
- "world update -- geopolitics, cyber, telecom, anything relevant"
- "brief me on what happened overnight"
- "I need my intelligence brief for today"
- "what's the global situation looking like this week"
- "quick world scan -- anything I should be worried about"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "create the intel briefing newsletter for subscribers" (intel-briefing-newsletter, public-facing)
- "write a market brief on the African DPI space" (market-brief skill)
- "what do we know about a competitor's latest moves" (competitor-intel or OSINT)
- "summarize this article for me" (general task, not world intelligence)
- "check my email for anything urgent" (sentinel or email task)
