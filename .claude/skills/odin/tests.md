# Test Prompts: odin

## Should Trigger (positive)

Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "Odin, what do you think about this deal structure?"
- "ask Odin about the pricing strategy"
- "what would Odin say about hiring a CRO now vs later"
- "Odin, study this https://youtube.com/watch?v=abc123"
- "Odin learn this article https://example.com/strategy-paper"
- "Odin, remember: in the Gulf, first meetings are never about business"
- "what does Odin know about negotiation tactics?"
- "Odin, think about this more"
- "Odin, review your positions on fundraising"
- "Odin, I just read a great book, study this: datastore/books/antifragile.pdf"
- "Odin, what's your take on going direct vs channel in Africa?"
- "Odin teach: our platform handles 400Gbps per node"
- "let me ask Odin about this before we decide"
- "Odin, is this a good deal or are we leaving money on the table?"
- "Odin, remember that Victor always needs to approve architecture decisions"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)

Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "think through the tradeoffs of build vs buy" (deep-think, no Odin address)
- "what's one more thing we should check" (numeral, not name)
- "add a note about this insight" (zk, not odin)
- "research the legacy incumbent for me" (osint, not odin)
- "ещё один вопрос" (Russian numeral "one more question", not Odin the advisor)
- "draft an email to the investor" (email-draft, not odin)
- "what do we know about ExampleTelco" (zk brief or osint, no Odin address)
- "prepare for the meeting with Sara" (meeting-prep, not odin)
- "I need to think about this carefully" (deep-think, no Odin address)
- "analyze this deal" (deal-strategy, no Odin address)
