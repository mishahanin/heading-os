# Test Prompts: meeting-prep

## Should Trigger (positive)
Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "prepare me for the call with ExampleTelco tomorrow at 2pm"
- "meeting prep for Nimbus Telecom -- I'm seeing their CTO next week"
- "what should I know before meeting Omar from ExampleTelco"
- "brief me on tomorrow's call with the Atlas Telecom procurement team"
- "I have a meeting with a potential investor in 2 hours, prep me"
- "get me ready for the Meridian Mobile pitch on Friday"
- "prep sheet for my 1:1 with Anthony this afternoon"
- "meeting with the UAE cybersecurity regulator -- what do I need to know"
- "I'm meeting Sam and the Nimbus Telecom guys tomorrow, prep me"
- "quick prep -- call with Leo Marsh in 30 min, what's his latest status"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "dig into the ExampleTelco CTO's background" (OSINT, not meeting prep)
- "how do we win the Nimbus Telecom deal" (deal-strategy, not meeting prep)
- "schedule a meeting with Sara for next Tuesday" (calendar task)
- "draft the follow-up email after the Atlas Telecom call" (follow-up or email skill)
- "what happened in yesterday's meeting" (event-debrief, not prep)
