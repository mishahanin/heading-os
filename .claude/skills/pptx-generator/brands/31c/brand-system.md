# 31 Concept - Brand System

> Sovereign deep packet intelligence. Dark, bold, authoritative. The visual language of a company that navigates by operational states, not goals.

---

## Brand Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Dark and Commanding**
   Pure black backgrounds. High-contrast white text. The visual equivalent of the captain's bridge at night - focused, clear, no distractions.

2. **Sovereign and Bold**
   Every visual choice communicates independence and authority. No borrowed aesthetics. No generic tech-startup look. 31C owns its visual space.

3. **Signal Over Noise**
   Every element earns its place. No decorative filler. If a shape, color, or word doesn't serve the message, it doesn't belong on the slide.

4. **Dual Accent System**
   Blue-purple (#5B5FFF) for primary emphasis - headings, product references, ODUN.ONE branding. Orange (#FF8C00) for secondary emphasis - highlights, call-outs, the signature top-left accent square.

## Logo

### The 31C Mark

A pixel/square-based "31C" wordmark. Clean, geometric, modern. The squares echo the digital/packet-level nature of the technology.

**Logo file:** `assets/31c-logo.png`

**Usage rules:**
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Minimum clear space: 20% of logo width on all sides
- On dark backgrounds: use light/blue-purple variant
- On light backgrounds: use dark variant
- Never distort, rotate, or add effects
- Position: bottom-left or top-right corner of slides

**Logo variants:**
- Primary: Blue-purple on transparent (for dark backgrounds)
- Dark: Black on transparent (for light backgrounds)

## Color System

### Primary Accent - 31C Blue-Purple

The signature brand color. Used for headings, ODUN.ONE references, decorative pixel squares, and primary emphasis.

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| 31C Blue-Purple | `#5B5FFF` | Headings, product accent, decorative squares |
| 31C Lavender | `#8B8FCC` | Content boxes, softer accents, tertiary elements |

**Rule:** Blue-purple is the hero accent. It marks what matters. Don't dilute it.

### Secondary Accent - 31C Orange

Bold, warm accent for highlights and call-outs. Creates visual contrast against the blue-purple.

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| 31C Orange | `#FF8C00` | Subheadings, highlights, top-left accent square, CTAs |

**Rule:** Orange is the attention-getter. Use it sparingly - one or two elements per slide maximum.

### Theme Base

```
Background:       #000000 (pure black)
Background Alt:   #0A0A14 (dark with subtle blue warmth)
Card Surface:     #12122A (dark blue-tinted surface)
Card Alt:         #1A1A35 (slightly lighter surface)
Code Background:  #0D0D15 (near-black for code blocks)
Text Primary:     #FFFFFF (pure white)
Text Secondary:   #B0B0C0 (muted silver)
```

## Typography

### Font Stack

- **Primary:** Inter - Clean, modern sans-serif for all text
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono - Technical content, code blocks

### Typography Rules

| Element | Font | Weight | Size | Line Height |
|---------|------|--------|------|-------------|
| Display/Hero | Inter | 700 (Bold) | 36px+ | 1.1 |
| H1 | Inter | 700 | 30px | 1.2 |
| H2 | Inter | 600 | 24px | 1.3 |
| H3 | Inter | 600 | 20px | 1.4 |
| Body | Inter | 400 | 16-18px | 1.6 |
| Small/Caption | Inter | 400 | 14px | 1.5 |
| Code | JetBrains Mono | 400 | 14px | 1.5 |

### Text Color Usage

| Context | Color | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Headings | `#FFFFFF` | Full contrast for emphasis |
| Body content | `#FFFFFF` | High readability on black |
| Secondary text | `#B0B0C0` | Muted for captions, labels |
| Accent text | `#5B5FFF` | Product names, key terms |
| Highlight text | `#FF8C00` | Critical numbers, call-outs |

## Signature Elements

### Orange Accent Square

A small orange (#FF8C00) square positioned in the top-left corner of slides. Signature 31C design element from the pitch deck.

**Specifications:**
- Size: ~20x20px or proportional
- Position: top-left corner, slight margin
- Present on: most slides (not title or closing slides)

### Scattered Pixel Squares

Small blue-purple (#5B5FFF) squares scattered decoratively on slides. Echoes the pixel/packet-level nature of the technology and mirrors the logo's geometric language.

**Usage:**
- 3-5 small squares per slide, scattered in margins or empty space
- Varying sizes (8px, 12px, 16px)
- Semi-transparent on some (60-80% opacity)
- Never obstruct content

### Dark Background Treatment

Pure black (#000000) as the primary background. For sections needing visual separation, use #0A0A14 or card surfaces (#12122A).

**Rule:** Never use light backgrounds. 31C is a dark-mode-only brand.

### Footer Pattern

Standard footer on all non-title slides:

```
(c) 2026 - 31 Concept | 31C.io | Proprietary & Confidential
```

**Note:** Use hyphen (-), never em-dash. Consistent with Misha's formatting rules.

## Slide Layout Patterns

### Title Slide
- Pure black background
- 31C logo centered or top-right
- Title in white, large Inter Bold
- Subtitle in #B0B0C0
- Orange accent square top-left
- No footer on title slides

### Content Slide
- Black background
- Orange accent square top-left
- Heading in white or #5B5FFF
- Body in white
- Card surfaces (#12122A) for grouping content
- Scattered blue-purple pixel squares in margins
- Footer at bottom

### Data/Stats Slide
- Key numbers in #FF8C00 (orange) for impact
- Labels in #B0B0C0
- Card backgrounds (#12122A) for each stat block
- Blue-purple accent for category labels

### Section Break
- Pure black background
- Section title in large white text
- Blue-purple decorative element
- Minimal - just the title and breathing room

## Quick Reference

### 31C Blue-Purple vs Orange

| Blue-Purple (#5B5FFF) | Orange (#FF8C00) |
|------------------------|-------------------|
| Headings and titles | Subheadings and highlights |
| Product names (ODUN.ONE) | Key statistics and numbers |
| Decorative pixel squares | Top-left accent square |
| Links and references | CTAs and action items |
| Primary emphasis | Secondary emphasis |

### What Belongs on a 31C Slide

- Clear hierarchy: one main message per slide
- Breathing room: 40% of the slide should be empty space
- Black background: always
- Maximum 6 lines of text (less is better)
- One accent color per element (don't mix blue-purple and orange on the same text)
