# Align - Worked Examples

Consumed by: `.claude/skills/align/SKILL.md` Phase 2 as a worked-output reference. Consult before composing the four-block Phase 2 response so the formatting, recommendation tone, and table shape match the established pattern.

Last Updated: 2026-06-10

Two examples below. Example A shows the all-lettered form. Example B shows the mixed open + lettered form. Both end with the canonical "Your call?" closer.

## Example A - lettered options + recommendations

User: `/align 3`
Prior request: "Write a launch email for the ODUN.ONE expo announcement"

Response:

```
Aligning against your request. Here's what I think you want:

You want a launch email announcing 31C's presence at a major expo with
ODUN.ONE on stage. Audience is the existing CRM (operators + partners).
The email should land before Day 1 of the event and drive booth visits.

## Question 1: Send window
a) Morning of Day 1 (event-day urgency, max open rate)
b) Two days before (lets recipients block calendar time)
c) Week before (gives partners time to schedule meetings)

My recommendation: b - two days before. Day-of email loses to event noise;
week-before is too early to feel urgent. Two days hits the planning sweet
spot for the partner segment.

## Question 2: Primary CTA
a) Book a private demo slot (calendar link)
b) Walk-in welcome at booth H4-37
c) Both - book or walk in

My recommendation: a - book a private demo. Walk-ins dilute the high-intent
signal; calendar bookings build the deal pipeline.

## Question 3: Length
a) Tight - 4 sentences + CTA
b) Medium - 80 words with one product line
c) Long - paragraph + bullets + signature

My recommendation: a - tight 4 sentences + CTA. Operators delete the rest.

| Q | Topic | Pick |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Send window | b |
| 2 | Primary CTA | a |
| 3 | Length | a |

Your call? Answer "1a, 2c, 3 - custom: ..., 4b, 5a" or "all my picks" to take every recommendation.
```

## Example B - mix of open + lettered

User: `/align 2` after a request "Schedule a meeting with Sara next week"

```
Aligning against your request. Here's what I think you want:

You want a 1:1 meeting with Sara Okonkwo on the Nimbus opportunity in the
week of 2026-05-19. Default is local time, video conference, 45 minutes.

## Question 1: Earliest acceptable day

What's the earliest day you'd take the meeting?

My recommendation: 2026-05-19 (Monday). Front-loading the week leaves
slippage room if Sara pushes.

## Question 2: Format
a) 30 min video on Teams
b) 45 min video on Teams
c) In-person at the Marina office (90 min, lunch slot)

My recommendation: b - 45 min Teams. Nimbus stage is mid-discovery; 30 min is
too short to land objections, in-person is overkill before the proposal.

| Q | Topic | Pick |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Earliest day | Mon 2026-05-19 |
| 2 | Format | b |

Your call? Answer "1: <day>, 2a/b/c" or "all my picks" to take every recommendation.
```
